Welcome!

WiFi Protected Setup (WPS) provides you an easier and faster way to connect your computers and wireless devices to your wireless network.

Push Button Configuration is one of the two methods you can use to connect your wireless devices to your home network.

 
Devices Scan

Connect new devices to the network and enable data encryption by pushing a button on the device. Access point/wireless router products will have a physical button, other devices may have a physical or software-based button.

In case a WPS-enabled device is found, a confirmation message will be shown in the interface of your wireless device.

 

Users should be aware that during the two-minute setup period which follows the push of the button, unintended devices could join the network if they are in range
